Jenni's Birthday

EECS 213

"Nothing brings people together like mutual struggle"

You remember how the year started and how I asked you out after brunch. Based off youre response I figured nothing would happen between us, but luckily we were in the same EECS 213 class. I was so releived when I asked if you had a partner and you said we should be partners. I was so worried I was going to be with some rando, but also, it meant that maybe things wouldn't have to be over between us. I'd have a whole quarter for us to get to know each other, and that made me happy. But god DAMM this class sucked. The homeworks were painfully tedious and difficult, but I still always looked forward to doing them, because it meant time with you. It was amazing getting to know you better and seeing how smart and passionate you were, though not passionate for 213. As they say, mutual struggle creates bonds, and that clearly holds for us.
