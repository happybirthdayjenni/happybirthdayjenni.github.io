Obviously we had a moment or two when we got frustrated with each other, but what is great about us is that we can get through those moments, and talk about them and grow from them. I love that I grow so much when I'm with you. I also get chills when I'm with you. But anyway, Canada was a great time and that was when I really felt like this is something really special as we grew closer over the week.
