Where it all (kinda) began

So this is where things were supposed to BEGIN begin. Things really started in the winter quarter, though. I saw you around at Sarge working between classes. It's probably pretty creepy to remember this, but i promise it's just because I have a good memory. Anyway, I thought you were pretty cute, but obviously you were studying I couldn't even say "hi". Then you came to a DX/DZ BP night and I played a game with you. I was on your team and asked if you were the person who did work at Sarge between classes. You were. When you left, I was talking to a couple other guys and said that you were really cute, and they all agreed emphatically, so naturally I thought you were well out of my league and not really worth a shot. Then, at winter formal, I didn't bring a date, and Emma said I should have brought her pledge daughter, and I thought "Oh yeah that'd be a great idea, I'll maybe do that for the next event". Then Relay rolled around and at this point most of my friends know I think you're really cute, so they egg me on to talk to you so I can ask you to Horse Prom. I of course fail to really do so, aside from I think one interaction. I remember overhearing you say you wouldn't change your last name if you get married, so I ask what your last name is and you say "Hutson". That was about that, moral of the story, I chickened out the first time we could've really interacted.
