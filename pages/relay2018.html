Where it all (kinda) began

So this is where things were supposed to BEGIN begin. Things really started in the winter quarter, though. I saw you around at Sarge working between classes. It's probably pretty creepy to remember this, but i promise it's just because I have a good memory. Anyway, I thought you were pretty cute, but obviously you were studying and I didn't want to interrupt. Then you came to a DX/DZ BP night and I played a game with you. I asked if you were the person who studied at Sarge between classes. You said you were. I was excited to finally have talked to you for a little bit. When you left, I was talking to a couple other guys and we all agreed you were really cute, so naturally I assumed you were well out of my league. Then, at winter formal, I didn't bring a date, and Emma said I should have brought her pledge daughter, and I thought "Oh yeah that'd be a great idea, I'll maybe do that for the next event". Then Relay rolled around and at this point most of my friends knew I think you're really cute, so they tried to get me to talk to you so I can ask you to Horse Prom. Of course I chicken out and don't really talk for you, aside from I think one interaction. I remember overhearing you say you wouldn't change your last name if you get married, so I ask what your last name is and you say "Hutson". That was about that, moral of the story, I chickened out the first time we could've really interacted.
